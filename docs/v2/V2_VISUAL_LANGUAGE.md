# BSDC FRONTEND V2 — VISUAL LANGUAGE

Status: LOCKED

---

## Philosophy

The experience should feel like:

- engineering map
- architectural plan
- technical exhibition
- physical route

The experience should NOT feel like:

- website
- template
- dashboard
- presentation

---

## Color System

Base Surface:

#8A9A86

Primary Text:

#1A221E

Secondary Text:

#4A5343

Depth Layer:

#242F2A

---

## Typography

Display:

Space Grotesk

UI:

Geist Mono

Body:

16–18px minimum

Readable at all times.

---

## Hero

Hero is a living environment.

Not a banner.

Not a title screen.

Not text on a background.

Hero must contain:

- atmosphere
- scale
- depth
- visual tension

Cursor adds life.

Cursor does not create life.

---

## About

About is spatial storytelling.

Information exists within a composition.

Not text blocks on a surface.

---

## Certificates

Documents feel physically placed.

Not arranged in grids.

---

## Partners

Partners behave as route markers.

Not logos in rows.

---

## Services

Services feel like a branching network.

Not cards.

Not tabs.

Not accordions.

---

## Projects

Projects feel like an archive system.

Not a gallery.

Not a blog.

---

## Contact

Contact feels like the final destination.

Large negative space.

Minimal distractions.

---

## Visual Priorities

Priority 1:

Space

Priority 2:

Composition

Priority 3:

Depth

Priority 4:

Content

Priority 5:

UI

---

## Forbidden Visual Patterns

- glassmorphism
- glow effects
- HUD overlays
- sci-fi graphics
- random particles
- scanlines
- decorative noise
- trendy web effects

---

## Desired User Reaction

"This feels like an interactive engineering environment."

Not:

"This looks like another company site."
